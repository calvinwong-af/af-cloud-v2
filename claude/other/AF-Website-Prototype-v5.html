Our Company

About AcceleFreight

A Malaysian freight forwarder built on the belief that logistics should be transparent, efficient, and accessible to every business.

Our Story

Established in 2018

AcceleFreight was founded in late 2018 with a clear goal: to improve how shipments in freight forwarding are managed for all parties — both customers and service providers.

We set out to bring greater visibility, faster response times, and reduced human error to the freight forwarding process — for customers of all sizes, with no additional cost.

AcceleFreight is a Malaysian incorporated freight forwarder and Multimodal Transport Operator (MTO). We provide services comparable to any established freight forwarder, with the added advantage of a dedicated team managing all your shipment transactions — so you only need to deal with us.

We currently operate in Malaysia and accept all shipments going in and out of the country, in cooperation with our trusted global partners. Our services are available exclusively to legally registered corporate and business entities.

What We Offer

Our Services

End-to-end freight and logistics solutions tailored for businesses operating in and out of Malaysia.

Sea Freight

Sea freight is the process of transporting cargo across the ocean on large cargo ships. We handle both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less Than Container Load (LCL) shipments, covering port-of-origin to port-of-destination and all logistics in between.

FCLLCLImportExport

Air Freight

For time-sensitive cargo, air freight offers the fastest transit times. Cargo is packaged in boxes, pallets, or standard containers and consolidated with other shipments to fill the aircraft. We handle port-to-port air shipments with full coordination from origin to destination.

ImportExportExpress

Cross-Border Trucking

Land freight transportation across international borders, including Malaysia–Singapore, Malaysia–Thailand, and other regional corridors. We handle all customs documentation, border clearance coordination, and final-mile handoff to ensure seamless cross-border cargo movement by road.

Malaysia–SingaporeMalaysia–ThailandRegional

Distribution Services

We provide last-mile and regional distribution services across Malaysia. This includes truck-load transportation on general trucks available in 1, 3, 5, and 10 ton sizes, and haulage services for moving containers to and from specified locations to ports or warehouses.

Last-mileHaulageTruck-load

Warehousing

We provide conventional warehousing for general goods storage and inventory management. Our facilities support a range of storage needs for businesses requiring reliable, secure holding of cargo before onward distribution or delivery.

General CargoStorageInventory

Cold Storage

For cargo with strict temperature requirements, our cold storage facilities maintain precise, monitored temperature control throughout the storage and handling process. Suitable for pharmaceuticals, perishable food products, and other temperature-sensitive goods.

Cold ChainPharmaPerishablesTemperature-Controlled

Customs Clearance

All cargo exported or imported across borders requires customs clearance. We handle all documentation preparation and submission, represent your company during customs examination, manage duty assessment and payment, and deliver cargo after clearance.

ImportExport

EOR / IOR Services

Exporter of Record (EOR) and Importer of Record (IOR) services allow businesses without a local legal entity in Malaysia to import or export goods compliantly. We act as the responsible party for all regulatory, tax, and customs obligations on your behalf.

EORIORCross-border

Outsourced Logistics Management & Consultation

For businesses looking to optimise their supply chain without building an in-house logistics team, we offer fully outsourced logistics management and expert consultation. We assess your operations, identify inefficiencies, and design tailored logistics solutions that reduce cost and improve reliability.

ManagementConsultationSupply Chain

We are currently focused on providing freight forwarding and warehousing services, along with supporting services such as distribution and customs clearance.

Generally, an appointment letter and supporting documents are required. Requirements may differ across countries. The general requirements in Malaysia are as follows:

  • An appointment letter on company letterhead, signed by a director with company stamp. We are happy to assist you prepare this.
  • Company incorporation/registration document (Form 9, Form D)
  • List of registered company directors (Form 49, Form A)
  • Proof of registered address (utility bill). If your registered address differs from your operating address, a tenancy agreement copy will be required.
  • Coloured photocopy of signed director's identification card

Some requirements may differ depending on your company's registration details. Contact us for more information — we are happy to help.
